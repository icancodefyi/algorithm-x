# Acceptable use of company systems

## Purpose

This policy defines permitted and prohibited use of company-issued devices, accounts, and networks. It applies to all employees, contractors, and guests using organizational IT resources.

## Permitted use

- Performing legitimate work duties using approved applications and accounts.
- Limited reasonable personal use that does not degrade security, performance, or compliance (e.g. brief personal email during breaks), unless local law or contract forbids it.

## Prohibited use

- Accessing, distributing, or storing illegal content.
- Circumventing security controls (VPN blocks, MFA, DLP, firewalls) without written Security approval.
- Running unauthorized cryptocurrency mining, botnets, or peer-to-peer file sharing on company assets.
- Sharing credentials or using another person’s account except where formally delegated with audit trail.

## Monitoring

The organization may log access to systems and networks for security, compliance, and investigation. Users should have no expectation of privacy on company-managed devices for work-related activity.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination and referral to law enforcement where appropriate.

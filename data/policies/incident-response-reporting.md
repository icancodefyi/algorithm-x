# Security incident reporting

## What counts as an incident

- Suspected malware, ransomware, or unauthorized access.
- Loss or theft of a device that can reach corporate data.
- Suspected phishing that led to credential entry or attachment execution.
- Accidental disclosure of confidential data to the wrong party.

## How to report

- **Primary channel:** email **security@company.example** or use the internal “Report security concern” form.
- **Urgent active attack** (e.g. ransomware in progress): call the **24/7 security hotline** published on the intranet and follow verbal instructions.

## What to include

- Time discovered, systems involved, user accounts touched, screenshots or email headers if safe to collect.
- **Do not** delete evidence or re-image machines without Security direction unless containment requires isolation (pull network cable / disable Wi‑Fi).

## User responsibilities

- Cooperate with investigation interviews and preserve logs as instructed.
- Retaliation against good-faith reporters is prohibited.

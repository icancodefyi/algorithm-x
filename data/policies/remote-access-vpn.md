# Remote access and VPN

## Scope

Rules for connecting to the corporate network from outside trusted office locations.

## Eligibility

Only users with a **business need** and **approved remote-work status** may use VPN. Contractors receive VPN only when contractually required and approved by the system owner.

## Configuration

- Company VPN client must be kept **up to date**; split tunneling follows the standard profile published by IT—users must not alter routing without approval.
- **Full tunnel** may be required when accessing crown-jewel applications from untrusted networks.

## Public and shared networks

- Use of **public Wi‑Fi** for sensitive work requires VPN; ideally use a personal hotspot when VPN is unavailable.
- Never disable host firewall or endpoint protection to “fix” connectivity without IT guidance.

## Session and device hygiene

- Lock screen when away; no unattended VPN sessions on shared machines.
- Report lost or stolen devices used for VPN within **24 hours** to IT and Security.
